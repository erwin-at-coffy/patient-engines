Yes. Our ad creative, intake language, and landing pages are written to the medical boards and platform policies for each service line. We've worked with practices on semaglutide, testosterone, and regenerative therapies without ad account strikes.
Will you lock us into a long contract?
First engagement is a 90-day build. After that, month-to-month. If the engine isn't moving the four lines we promised — booked, shown, started, retained — you walk, no penalty.
What do you actually charge?
Engagements range $8k–$25k/mo depending on service lines, locations, and whether you need the CRM and conversion system rebuilt. Ad spend is separate and paid directly to the platforms. We'll quote precisely on a fit call.
How is this different from a typical agency?
Typical agencies report leads. We report revenue. The retainer isn't for ads — it's for an operating system that makes self-pay medicine predictable. The ad account is one part of four.
Can you work with my existing CRM and front desk team?
Yes. We've built on top of Boulevard, Aesthetic Record, Weave, GoHighLevel, Zenoti, and custom stacks. We train the front desk on the new cadences and sit in on the first four weeks of morning huddles.
How soon do we see results?
First qualified bookings land in week 2–3. The revenue line shifts meaningfully in month 2. Membership enrollment and plan value compound through month 3–6.
